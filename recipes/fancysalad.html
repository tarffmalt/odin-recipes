Fancy Salad

Fancy Salad, as dubbed by Nissan R., is a flavorful and satisfying way to eat parsley and lots of nut protein.

While there are a number of steps to prepare Fancy Salad, the effort is worthwhile.

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch parsley
  • 1/2 cup almonds, chopped
  • 1/3 cup pumpkin seeds
  • 1/3 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1 pomegranate, cleaned
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1.5 lemons, juiced
  • Sea salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat dry pan and add chopped almonds
  2. After 1 minute add pumplin and sunflower seeds
  3. Stir gently on medium heat
  4. While nuds and seeds roast, remove and discard bottom stems of parsley and chop the rest
  5. Add chopped parsley to a salad bowl
  6. Once almonds are golden brown and pumpkin seeds have stopped popping, pour into salad bowl
  7. Add pomegrante seeds
  8. Season with salt and pepper
  9. Add lemon juice
  10. Add olive oil
  11. Toss and serve
